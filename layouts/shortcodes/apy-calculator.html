APY (Atal Pension Yojana) is a government-guaranteed pension for unorganised sector workers. Entry age 18-40. Pension starts at age 60. On death, the spouse continues to receive the same pension. Government may co-contribute 50% of contribution (max ₹1,000/yr) for eligible subscribers.
Tax benefit: Contributions qualify for Section 80CCD(1B) deduction, up to ₹50,000 over and above the 80C limit of ₹1.5 lakh.
