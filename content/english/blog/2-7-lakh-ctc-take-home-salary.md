Karthik started at a ₹2.7 lakh lab technician role in Madurai. The offer letter said ₹22,500 a month. The bank credit was ₹19,059. The whole gap is your own EPF, the ₹2,400 professional tax, and the employer PF plus gratuity that live inside CTC and never reach you. In-hand at 2.7 LPA works out to about 85% of CTC.

## What ₹2.7 lakh CTC actually contains

Standard 50% basic structure at ₹2.7L:

| Component | Annual | Monthly |
|---|---|---|
| Basic salary | ₹1,35,000 | ₹11,250 |
| HRA (50% of basic) | ₹67,500 | ₹5,625 |
| Special allowance | ₹44,806 | ₹3,734 |
| Employer PF (12% of basic) | ₹16,200 | ₹1,350 |
| Gratuity provision (4.81%) | ₹6,494 | ₹541 |
| **Total CTC** | **₹2,70,000** | **₹22,500** |

Employer PF plus gratuity comes to ₹22,694. That money sits inside the ₹2.7 lakh CTC and never lands in your salary account. Your gross salary, the part payroll actually pays, is ₹2,47,306 a year.

## 2.7 LPA in-hand salary: the take-home math

| Item | Annual | Monthly |
|---|---|---|
| Gross salary (excl. employer PF + gratuity) | ₹2,47,306 | ₹20,609 |
| Less: Employee PF | ₹16,200 | ₹1,350 |
| Less: Professional tax (Karnataka) | ₹2,400 | ₹200 |
| Less: Income tax | ₹0 | ₹0 |
| **In-hand** | **₹2,28,706** | **₹19,059** |

Taxable income after the ₹75,000 standard deduction is ₹1,72,306, and the 87A rebate wipes out any tax the slabs produce. So income tax is zero. The only money leaving your gross is your own PF of ₹16,200 and ₹2,400 of professional tax.

## Why in-hand is about 85% of CTC here

Your in-hand at ₹2.7 lakh is ₹2,28,706, roughly 85% of CTC. Early on almost everything you earn is yours, which is exactly when a savings habit is cheapest to start. The share reaching your hand only drops from here: about 80% near ₹16 lakh, 75% near ₹30 lakh, under 60% at ₹1 crore.

The ₹1,350 a month into PF is part of that gap. Your employer adds the same again, so close to ₹2,700 a month is quietly compounding for you. Track it with the [EPF calculator](/salary/epf-calculator/).
