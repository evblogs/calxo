Aditya joined at a ₹4.6 lakh associate consultant role in Bengaluru. The offer letter said ₹38,333 a month. The bank credit was ₹32,611. The whole gap is your own EPF, the ₹2,400 professional tax, and the employer PF plus gratuity that live inside CTC and never reach you. In-hand at 4.6 LPA works out to about 85% of CTC.

## What ₹4.6 lakh CTC actually contains

Standard 50% basic structure at ₹4.6L:

| Component | Annual | Monthly |
|---|---|---|
| Basic salary | ₹2,30,000 | ₹19,167 |
| HRA (50% of basic) | ₹1,15,000 | ₹9,583 |
| Special allowance | ₹76,337 | ₹6,361 |
| Employer PF (12% of basic) | ₹27,600 | ₹2,300 |
| Gratuity provision (4.81%) | ₹11,063 | ₹922 |
| **Total CTC** | **₹4,60,000** | **₹38,333** |

Employer PF plus gratuity comes to ₹38,663. That money sits inside the ₹4.6 lakh CTC and never lands in your salary account. Your gross salary, the part payroll actually pays, is ₹4,21,337 a year.

## 4.6 LPA in-hand salary: the take-home math

| Item | Annual | Monthly |
|---|---|---|
| Gross salary (excl. employer PF + gratuity) | ₹4,21,337 | ₹35,111 |
| Less: Employee PF | ₹27,600 | ₹2,300 |
| Less: Professional tax (Karnataka) | ₹2,400 | ₹200 |
| Less: Income tax | ₹0 | ₹0 |
| **In-hand** | **₹3,91,337** | **₹32,611** |

Taxable income after the ₹75,000 standard deduction is ₹3,46,337, and the 87A rebate wipes out any tax the slabs produce. So income tax is zero. The only money leaving your gross is your own PF of ₹27,600 and ₹2,400 of professional tax.

## Why in-hand is about 85% of CTC here

Your in-hand at ₹4.6 lakh is ₹3,91,337, roughly 85% of CTC. Early on almost everything you earn is yours, which is exactly when a savings habit is cheapest to start. The share reaching your hand only drops from here: about 80% near ₹16 lakh, 75% near ₹30 lakh, under 60% at ₹1 crore.

The ₹2,300 a month into PF is part of that gap. Your employer adds the same again, so close to ₹4,600 a month is quietly compounding for you. Track it with the [EPF calculator](/salary/epf-calculator/).
