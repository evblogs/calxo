## What 5 Lakh CTC Actually Means

A 5 lakh CTC sounds like ₹41,667 a month. You will not receive ₹41,667. The number that hits your account is closer to ₹37,000, and understanding why takes about two minutes.

Your employer adds multiple components to arrive at the CTC figure: basic salary, HRA, special allowance, and their share of EPF and gratuity. Only some of those components are actually paid to you as cash.

### The Typical Salary Breakdown at 5 LPA

| Component | Annual (₹) | Monthly (₹) |
|---|---|---|
| Basic Pay | 2,50,000 | 20,833 |
| House Rent Allowance | 1,25,000 | 10,417 |
| Special Allowance | 91,375 | 7,615 |
| Employer PF (your CTC, not cash) | 21,600 | 1,800 |
| Gratuity (accrues, not monthly cash) | 12,025 | 1,002 |
| **Total CTC** | **5,00,000** | **41,667** |

### What Gets Deducted

Employee PF is 12% of basic pay. At ₹2,50,000 annual basic, that is ₹30,000 per year. But the EPF ceiling on employer contributions is ₹21,600 per year, so employee contribution mirrors at ₹21,600.

Income tax on 5L CTC is zero. The 87A rebate wipes out any tax liability for taxable incomes up to ₹7 lakh in the new regime. After the ₹75,000 standard deduction, your taxable income here is under ₹4 lakh. No tax.

Professional tax depends on your state, typically ₹2,400 per year maximum.

### The Actual In-Hand Figure

Annual take-home = Gross salary (₹4,66,375) minus employee PF (₹21,600) minus income tax (₹0) = ₹4,44,775.

Monthly in-hand: roughly **₹37,000 to ₹37,500**, assuming default salary structure and new tax regime.

My colleague Priya joined a Pune startup at 5 LPA and was puzzled when her first salary credit was ₹36,900 instead of ₹41,667. It is not a payroll error. The PF deduction alone accounts for ₹1,800 per month, and the employer PF and gratuity components were never going to be cash.

### Maximizing Take-Home at This Level

The main lever you have is professional tax planning. You cannot reduce PF contributions at this salary band without your employer agreeing. But choosing the new tax regime over the old one is the right call at 5 LPA since the rebate under the new regime gives you zero tax liability.

If your company offers a flexible benefit plan, load up food coupons or fuel allowance. These are not taxable up to certain limits and directly raise your effective cash in hand each month.
