Calxo's GST calculators are built for the actual workflow of an Indian small business — raising invoices, reconciling purchase bills, working out HSN-coded rates, and handling intra-state versus inter-state splits without thinking about the formula.

## What you can calculate here

- **Add GST** to any base price across the 0%, 0.25%, 3%, 5%, 12%, 18% and 28% slabs
- **Reverse / remove GST** from a tax-inclusive total to find the base value (the formula most people get wrong)
- **CGST + SGST split** for intra-state transactions
- **IGST** for inter-state and export-with-tax transactions

## Why these are different

Most GST calculators online are generic tax-percentage tools repackaged for the Indian market. Calxo's tools handle the specifics — the correct reverse formula `Base = Total × 100 / (100 + Rate)`, the equal CGST/SGST split, the proper IGST handling for inter-state, and the actual current Indian slabs from the GST Council.
