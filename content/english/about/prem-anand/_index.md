Prem Anand reviews every calculator on Calxo before it goes live. His role is specific: check that the formula matches the actual statute, confirm the numbers against published rates from RBI, CBDT, AMFI, and EPFO, and flag any scenario where the calculator output could mislead someone making a real financial decision.

His review covers the logic layer, not the copy. So for the EMI calculator, that means verifying the reducing-balance formula matches the RBI Master Direction on interest calculation. For the income tax calculator, it means running the new and old regime slabs against CBDT's FY 2025-26 notification and checking that marginal relief and surcharge thresholds are handled correctly.

Calxo's calculators are reference tools, not financial advice. Prem's review ensures the reference is accurate. If you find a number that looks wrong, email calxo directly. Discrepancies get escalated to him for verification within 48 hours.
