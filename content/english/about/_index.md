## Why we built Calxo

Most calculator sites are either generic global tools using dollars and US tax rules, or buried inside bank/insurance landing pages that exist to sell you something. Indian users searching for an EMI, SIP, or HRA calculator deserve fast, accurate, India-specific tools that don't ask for a phone number.

Calxo is a free, ad-light calculator hub built for Indian users. Every tool uses Indian rules — INR, GST slabs, current income tax regimes (old and new), 80C limits, EPF rules, and the formulas the RBI and Income Tax Department actually publish.

## What we cover

- **Loan calculators** — EMI, home loan, personal loan, car loan, student loan
- **Investment calculators** — SIP, lumpsum, FD, RD, PPF, NPS, mutual fund SWP
- **Tax calculators** — Income tax (old & new regime), capital gains, advance tax
- **Salary & HR** — CTC to in-hand, gratuity, HRA exemption, leave encashment
- **GST** — Inclusive, exclusive, reverse calculation, slab-wise
- **Conversions** — Unit, length, area, currency, time

## How we work

Every calculator is built from the official source (RBI circulars, Income Tax Department notifications, CBDT rules). We update tools the same week tax slabs or interest rates change. No formulas are made up. Every result page links to the rule or section it implements.

We don't store your inputs. Every calculation runs in your browser.
