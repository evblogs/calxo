## Why Calxo exists

Most Indian calculator sites are one of two things: generic global tools using dollars and US tax rules, or buried inside a bank or insurance landing page that exists to capture your phone number. Both are useless if you just want to figure out an EMI before deciding whether to take a home loan.

Calxo is a free, ad-light calculator hub for Indian users. Every tool uses Indian rules: INR, current GST slabs, both income tax regimes, 80C limits, EPF rules, and the formulas the RBI and Income Tax Department actually publish. No sign-up, no phone number, no email opt-in to see your result.

## What we cover

- **Loan calculators**: EMI, home loan, personal loan, car loan, education loan, bike loan, stamp duty, FOIR
- **Investment calculators**: SIP, lumpsum, FD, RD, PPF, NPS, compound interest, NPS Vatsalya
- **Tax calculators**: Income tax (old and new regime), capital gains, TDS, freelancer tax under 44ADA
- **Salary and HR**: CTC to in-hand, HRA exemption, gratuity, EPF, salary hike, salary to hourly rate
- **GST**: Inclusive and exclusive GST, reverse calculation, lifetime vehicle tax
- **Health**: BMI, TDEE, body fat percentage, water intake, calorie deficit
- **Math**: Percentage, margin, discount, age, date calculators
- **Conversions**: Live currency converter, petrol price by city

## How we work

Every calculator is built from the official source: RBI circulars, Income Tax Department notifications, CBDT rules, GST Council releases. We update tools the same week tax slabs or interest rates change. No formulas are made up. Every result page links to the rule or section it implements.

We don't store your inputs. Every calculation runs in your browser. We use Google Analytics 4 for anonymous traffic stats, nothing else.

This is not financial advice. For decisions that affect your money in a serious way, consult a SEBI-registered advisor or a Chartered Accountant. Calxo is the back-of-envelope tool that helps you walk into that conversation already knowing the math.
