**Last updated: April 25, 2026**

## What we collect

- **Calculator inputs:** None. Every calculation runs entirely in your browser. We do not transmit, log, or store the numbers you enter.
- **Analytics:** We use Google Analytics 4 to understand which calculators are most useful. GA4 sees anonymised page views, country, device type, and referrer. It does not see your calculator inputs or results.
- **Search queries:** When you use the on-site search, the query stays in your browser. We do not log search terms server-side.

## Cookies

We use only essential cookies (session, theme preference) and the GA4 cookie. You can block these in your browser without breaking the site.

## Third-party services

- **Google Analytics 4**: anonymised traffic stats
- **Netlify**: site hosting; sees standard request logs (IP, user agent) for security
- **Google Fonts**: loaded from fonts.googleapis.com

We do not use ad networks, retargeting pixels, or session-replay tools.

## Your rights

Because we don't collect personal data, there's nothing to delete or export. If you've contacted us by email, you can ask us to delete that email thread at any time.
