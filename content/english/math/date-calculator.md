## Three things this calculator does

**Days between dates** — pick any two dates and instantly see the gap in days, weeks, months, and working days. Useful for contract periods, notice periods, loan tenures, and anything with a fixed start and end.

**Add or subtract days** — start from any date and jump forward or backward. Handy for figuring out what date falls 90 days from today, or when a 30-day return window closes.

**Countdown** — set a target date and see how many days, weeks, and working days remain. Use it for exam deadlines, visa expiry dates, project submissions.

## Working days vs calendar days

The working days count excludes Saturdays and Sundays. It does not automatically exclude public holidays since holidays vary by state and organisation in India. Subtract public holidays manually if you need a precise business-day count.

## Common situations

Notice period: most Indian companies require 30–90 days notice. Check what date your last working day falls on by adding days to your resignation date.

Loan tenure: an EMI loan of 36 months from 1 June 2026 — this tool tells you the exact end date. Use the [EMI Calculator](/loan/emi-calculator/) alongside to see your monthly payment.

Visa validity: know exactly how many days remain before your visa expires.
