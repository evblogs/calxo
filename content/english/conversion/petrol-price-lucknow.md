## What makes up the petrol price in Lucknow

Every litre of petrol in Lucknow carries two central charges that are the same across India, plus Uttar Pradesh's own state tax on top. Uttar Pradesh levies approximately 19.36% or ₹14.85/L (higher) on petrol, and that state slab is the single biggest reason Lucknow prices differ from a city like Delhi.

| Component | Amount (₹/litre) |
|---|---|
| Base price (ex-refinery) | ~56.00 |
| Central excise duty | 19.90 |
| **Uttar Pradesh state tax (VAT/cess)** | **~22.09** |
| Dealer commission | ~3.87 |
| **Total pump price** | **~101.86** |

The base price and excise duty are fixed nationally. State tax is the variable layer, so two cities can sell the same fuel at very different pump prices purely because of state politics.

## Frequently asked questions

### When does the petrol price change in Lucknow?

Indian Oil, BPCL and HPCL revise fuel prices at 6:00 AM every day. The Lucknow rate shown here is refreshed daily from official oil-company data. Day-to-day moves are usually in paise unless there is a sharp swing in global crude or a change in Uttar Pradesh VAT.

### Is petrol cheaper in Noida or Lucknow?

Right now Lucknow is ₹101.86/litre and Noida is around ₹103.13. The difference comes down to state tax, since both cities draw fuel from the same oil marketing companies.

### How do I check the live petrol price in Lucknow?

You can use the Indian Oil, BPCL or HPCL apps, or IOCL's SMS service: send "RSP" to 9224992249. The prices on this page are updated daily from official OMC data.
