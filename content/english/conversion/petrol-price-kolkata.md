## What makes up the petrol price in Kolkata

Every litre of petrol in Kolkata carries two central charges that are the same across India, plus West Bengal's own state tax on top. West Bengal levies approximately 25% + ₹1,000 cess on petrol, and that state slab is the single biggest reason Kolkata prices differ from a city like Delhi.

| Component | Amount (₹/litre) |
|---|---|
| Base price (ex-refinery) | ~56.00 |
| Central excise duty | 19.90 |
| **West Bengal state tax (VAT/cess)** | **~25.18** |
| Dealer commission | ~3.87 |
| **Total pump price** | **~104.95** |

The base price and excise duty are fixed nationally. State tax is the variable layer, so two cities can sell the same fuel at very different pump prices purely because of state politics.

## Frequently asked questions

### When does the petrol price change in Kolkata?

Indian Oil, BPCL and HPCL revise fuel prices at 6:00 AM every day. The Kolkata rate shown here is refreshed daily from official oil-company data. Day-to-day moves are usually in paise unless there is a sharp swing in global crude or a change in West Bengal VAT.

### Is petrol cheaper in Bhubaneswar or Kolkata?

Right now Kolkata is ₹104.95/litre and Bhubaneswar is around ₹101.66. The difference comes down to state tax, since both cities draw fuel from the same oil marketing companies.

### How do I check the live petrol price in Kolkata?

You can use the Indian Oil, BPCL or HPCL apps, or IOCL's SMS service: send "RSP" to 9224992249. The prices on this page are updated daily from official OMC data.
