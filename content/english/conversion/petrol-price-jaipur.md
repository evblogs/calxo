## What makes up the petrol price in Jaipur

Every litre of petrol in Jaipur carries two central charges that are the same across India, plus Rajasthan's own state tax on top. Rajasthan levies approximately 29.03% on petrol, and that state slab is the single biggest reason Jaipur prices differ from a city like Delhi.

| Component | Amount (₹/litre) |
|---|---|
| Base price (ex-refinery) | ~56.00 |
| Central excise duty | 19.90 |
| **Rajasthan state tax (VAT/cess)** | **~33.42** |
| Dealer commission | ~3.87 |
| **Total pump price** | **~113.19** |

The base price and excise duty are fixed nationally. State tax is the variable layer, so two cities can sell the same fuel at very different pump prices purely because of state politics.

## Frequently asked questions

### When does the petrol price change in Jaipur?

Indian Oil, BPCL and HPCL revise fuel prices at 6:00 AM every day. The Jaipur rate shown here is refreshed daily from official oil-company data. Day-to-day moves are usually in paise unless there is a sharp swing in global crude or a change in Rajasthan VAT.

### Is petrol cheaper in Delhi or Jaipur?

Right now Jaipur is ₹113.19/litre and Delhi is around ₹102.12. The difference comes down to state tax, since both cities draw fuel from the same oil marketing companies.

### How do I check the live petrol price in Jaipur?

You can use the Indian Oil, BPCL or HPCL apps, or IOCL's SMS service: send "RSP" to 9224992249. The prices on this page are updated daily from official OMC data.
