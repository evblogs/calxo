## What makes up the petrol price in Bhubaneswar

Every litre of petrol in Bhubaneswar carries two central charges that are the same across India, plus Odisha's own state tax on top. Odisha levies approximately 28% on petrol, and that state slab is the single biggest reason Bhubaneswar prices differ from a city like Delhi.

| Component | Amount (₹/litre) |
|---|---|
| Base price (ex-refinery) | ~56.00 |
| Central excise duty | 19.90 |
| **Odisha state tax (VAT/cess)** | **~29.04** |
| Dealer commission | ~3.87 |
| **Total pump price** | **~108.81** |

The base price and excise duty are fixed nationally. State tax is the variable layer, so two cities can sell the same fuel at very different pump prices purely because of state politics.

## Frequently asked questions

### When does the petrol price change in Bhubaneswar?

Indian Oil, BPCL and HPCL revise fuel prices at 6:00 AM every day. The Bhubaneswar rate shown here is refreshed daily from official oil-company data. Day-to-day moves are usually in paise unless there is a sharp swing in global crude or a change in Odisha VAT.

### Is petrol cheaper in Visakhapatnam or Bhubaneswar?

Right now Bhubaneswar is ₹108.81/litre and Visakhapatnam is around ₹116.50. The difference comes down to state tax, since both cities draw fuel from the same oil marketing companies.

### How do I check the live petrol price in Bhubaneswar?
