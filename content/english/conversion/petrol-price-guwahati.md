## What makes up the petrol price in Guwahati

Every litre of petrol in Guwahati carries two central charges that are the same across India, plus Assam's own state tax on top. Assam levies approximately 32.66% or ₹22.63/L (higher) on petrol, and that state slab is the single biggest reason Guwahati prices differ from a city like Delhi.

| Component | Amount (₹/litre) |
|---|---|
| Base price (ex-refinery) | ~56.00 |
| Central excise duty | 19.90 |
| **Assam state tax (VAT/cess)** | **~26.88** |
| Dealer commission | ~3.87 |
| **Total pump price** | **~106.65** |

The base price and excise duty are fixed nationally. State tax is the variable layer, so two cities can sell the same fuel at very different pump prices purely because of state politics.

## Frequently asked questions

### When does the petrol price change in Guwahati?

Indian Oil, BPCL and HPCL revise fuel prices at 6:00 AM every day. The Guwahati rate shown here is refreshed daily from official oil-company data. Day-to-day moves are usually in paise unless there is a sharp swing in global crude or a change in Assam VAT.

### Is petrol cheaper in Patna or Guwahati?

Right now Guwahati is ₹106.65/litre and Patna is around ₹114.36. The difference comes down to state tax, since both cities draw fuel from the same oil marketing companies.

### How do I check the live petrol price in Guwahati?

You can use the Indian Oil, BPCL or HPCL apps, or IOCL's SMS service: send "RSP" to 9224992249. The prices on this page are updated daily from official OMC data.
