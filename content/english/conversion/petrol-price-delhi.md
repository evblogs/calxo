## Why Delhi has cheap petrol vs other metros

Petrol price has two fixed layers across India and one variable layer:

| Component | Amount (₹/litre) |
|---|---|
| Base price (ex-refinery) | ~56.00 |
| Central excise duty | 19.90 |
| **Delhi state VAT (~19.4%)** | **~11.50** |
| Dealer commission | ~3.87 |
| **Total pump price** | **~94.77** |

Maharashtra (where Mumbai is) charges ~26% state VAT on petrol. Telangana (Hyderabad) charges ~35% VAT. This is why Delhi saves ₹8–13/litre vs those cities. Same fuel, same central taxes — different state politics.

## Frequently asked questions

### When do petrol prices change in Delhi?

Indian Oil, BPCL, and HPCL revise fuel prices at 6:00 AM every day. However, prices in Delhi haven't changed since May 2022 — the last national revision. Any change would require either a central excise cut or a state VAT revision by the Delhi government.

### Is petrol cheaper in Noida or Delhi?

Almost identical. Noida (UP) is ₹94.71/litre vs Delhi's ₹94.77. UP has nearly the same VAT as Delhi. Gurgaon (Haryana) is ₹94.30–₹95.50 depending on the specific location.

### How can I check real-time petrol price in Delhi?

You can check on the Indian Oil, BPCL, or HPCL apps. IOCL has an SMS service: SMS "RSP" to 9224992249. Our prices are updated daily from official OMC data.
