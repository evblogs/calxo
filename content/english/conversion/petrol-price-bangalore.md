## Frequently asked questions

### Where is petrol cheapest in Bangalore?

Prices are uniform across all Indian Oil, BPCL, and HPCL pumps within Bangalore city. The only variation is if you drive to the outskirts into a different district — but state VAT applies statewide in Karnataka, so prices are the same across the state.

### Does the CNG price in Bangalore affect petrol demand?

Yes. BMTC buses run on CNG and diesel. For private car owners, CNG retrofit options exist but aren't as widespread as in Delhi or Mumbai. Most Bangaloreans on petrol vehicles either continue on petrol or switch directly to EVs — CNG is a smaller middle ground here.

### What is today's diesel price in Bangalore?

Today's diesel price in Bangalore is ₹88.60 per litre (as of 29 April 2026). Diesel is cheaper than petrol in every Indian city because the central government applies a lower excise duty on diesel.
