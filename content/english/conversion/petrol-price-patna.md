## What makes up the petrol price in Patna

Every litre of petrol in Patna carries two central charges that are the same across India, plus Bihar's own state tax on top. Bihar levies approximately 23.58% + irrecoverable tax on petrol, and that state slab is the single biggest reason Patna prices differ from a city like Delhi.

| Component | Amount (₹/litre) |
|---|---|
| Base price (ex-refinery) | ~56.00 |
| Central excise duty | 19.90 |
| **Bihar state tax (VAT/cess)** | **~34.78** |
| Dealer commission | ~3.87 |
| **Total pump price** | **~114.55** |

The base price and excise duty are fixed nationally. State tax is the variable layer, so two cities can sell the same fuel at very different pump prices purely because of state politics.

## Frequently asked questions

### When does the petrol price change in Patna?

Indian Oil, BPCL and HPCL revise fuel prices at 6:00 AM every day. The Patna rate shown here is refreshed daily from official oil-company data. Day-to-day moves are usually in paise unless there is a sharp swing in global crude or a change in Bihar VAT.

### Is petrol cheaper in Ranchi or Patna?

Right now Patna is ₹114.55/litre and Ranchi is around ₹106.19. The difference comes down to state tax, since both cities draw fuel from the same oil marketing companies.

### How do I check the live petrol price in Patna?

You can use the Indian Oil, BPCL or HPCL apps, or IOCL's SMS service: send "RSP" to 9224992249. The prices on this page are updated daily from official OMC data.
