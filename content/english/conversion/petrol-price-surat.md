## What makes up the petrol price in Surat

Every litre of petrol in Surat carries two central charges that are the same across India, plus Gujarat's own state tax on top. Gujarat levies approximately 13.7% VAT + 4% cess on petrol, and that state slab is the single biggest reason Surat prices differ from a city like Delhi.

| Component | Amount (₹/litre) |
|---|---|
| Base price (ex-refinery) | ~56.00 |
| Central excise duty | 19.90 |
| **Gujarat state tax (VAT/cess)** | **~13.71** |
| Dealer commission | ~3.87 |
| **Total pump price** | **~93.48** |

The base price and excise duty are fixed nationally. State tax is the variable layer, so two cities can sell the same fuel at very different pump prices purely because of state politics.

## Frequently asked questions

### When does the petrol price change in Surat?

Indian Oil, BPCL and HPCL revise fuel prices at 6:00 AM every day. The Surat rate shown here is refreshed daily from official oil-company data. Day-to-day moves are usually in paise unless there is a sharp swing in global crude or a change in Gujarat VAT.

### Is petrol cheaper in Vadodara or Surat?

Right now Surat is ₹93.48/litre and Vadodara is around ₹93.30. The difference comes down to state tax, since both cities draw fuel from the same oil marketing companies.

### How do I check the live petrol price in Surat?

You can use the Indian Oil, BPCL or HPCL apps, or IOCL's SMS service: send "RSP" to 9224992249. The prices on this page are updated daily from official OMC data.
