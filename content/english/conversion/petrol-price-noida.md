## What makes up the petrol price in Noida

Every litre of petrol in Noida carries two central charges that are the same across India, plus Uttar Pradesh's own state tax on top. Uttar Pradesh levies approximately 19.36% or ₹14.85/L (higher) on petrol, and that state slab is the single biggest reason Noida prices differ from a city like Delhi.

| Component | Amount (₹/litre) |
|---|---|
| Base price (ex-refinery) | ~56.00 |
| Central excise duty | 19.90 |
| **Uttar Pradesh state tax (VAT/cess)** | **~14.94** |
| Dealer commission | ~3.87 |
| **Total pump price** | **~94.71** |

The base price and excise duty are fixed nationally. State tax is the variable layer, so two cities can sell the same fuel at very different pump prices purely because of state politics.

## Frequently asked questions

### When does the petrol price change in Noida?

Indian Oil, BPCL and HPCL revise fuel prices at 6:00 AM every day. The Noida rate shown here is refreshed daily from official oil-company data. Day-to-day moves are usually in paise unless there is a sharp swing in global crude or a change in Uttar Pradesh VAT.

### Is petrol cheaper in Delhi or Noida?

Right now Noida is ₹94.71/litre and Delhi is around ₹94.77. The difference comes down to state tax, since both cities draw fuel from the same oil marketing companies.

### How do I check the live petrol price in Noida?

You can use the Indian Oil, BPCL or HPCL apps, or IOCL's SMS service: send "RSP" to 9224992249. The prices on this page are updated daily from official OMC data.
