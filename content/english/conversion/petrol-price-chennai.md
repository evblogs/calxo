## Frequently asked questions

### What is today's diesel price in Chennai?

Today's diesel price in Chennai is ₹92.34 per litre (as of 18 June 2026). Note that Chennai's diesel is among the more expensive in India — Tamil Nadu's cess on diesel is relatively high compared to states like Chandigarh (₹83.02) or Gujarat (₹88.07).

### How do I check petrol price in Chennai tomorrow?

Prices are revised at 6:00 AM daily. Check this page after 6 AM for the updated price. You can also check the IOCL website, BPCL app, or send an SMS "RSP" to 9224992249 (Indian Oil).

### Does Chennai have CNG pumps?

CNG infrastructure in Chennai is limited compared to Delhi or Mumbai. IGL and MNGL don't operate in Chennai; the city runs mostly on petrol, diesel, and LPG for autos. EV adoption is growing faster than CNG conversion in Chennai.
