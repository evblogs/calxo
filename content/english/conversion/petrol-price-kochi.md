## What makes up the petrol price in Kochi

Every litre of petrol in Kochi carries two central charges that are the same across India, plus Kerala's own state tax on top. Kerala levies approximately 30.08% + ₹1/L cess on petrol, and that state slab is the single biggest reason Kochi prices differ from a city like Delhi.

| Component | Amount (₹/litre) |
|---|---|
| Base price (ex-refinery) | ~56.00 |
| Central excise duty | 19.90 |
| **Kerala state tax (VAT/cess)** | **~27.37** |
| Dealer commission | ~3.87 |
| **Total pump price** | **~107.14** |

The base price and excise duty are fixed nationally. State tax is the variable layer, so two cities can sell the same fuel at very different pump prices purely because of state politics.

## Frequently asked questions

### When does the petrol price change in Kochi?

Indian Oil, BPCL and HPCL revise fuel prices at 6:00 AM every day. The Kochi rate shown here is refreshed daily from official oil-company data. Day-to-day moves are usually in paise unless there is a sharp swing in global crude or a change in Kerala VAT.

### Is petrol cheaper in Coimbatore or Kochi?

Right now Kochi is ₹107.14/litre and Coimbatore is around ₹100.72. The difference comes down to state tax, since both cities draw fuel from the same oil marketing companies.

### How do I check the live petrol price in Kochi?

You can use the Indian Oil, BPCL or HPCL apps, or IOCL's SMS service: send "RSP" to 9224992249. The prices on this page are updated daily from official OMC data.
