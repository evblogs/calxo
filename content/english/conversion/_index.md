Calxo's conversion calculators handle the everyday number-to-number conversions that come up in cross-border life: live currency exchange for NRIs, freelancers and importers; unit conversion for online shopping and recipes; and other format-shifters that don't need an app.

## What you can convert here

- **Currency** — 20+ currencies relevant to Indian users (USD, EUR, GBP, AED, SGD, AUD, CAD, JPY, SAR, QAR, more) with live mid-market rates
- **Length, weight, temperature, area, volume** — coming soon, all in metric and imperial

## Why these are different

Most generic converters online use stale rate tables or are wrappers over an unreliable API with no offline behaviour. The Calxo currency converter pulls live rates from a free public source (open.er-api.com), and falls back gracefully to indicative rates with a clear warning if the live fetch fails — so you always get a usable number.

For currency planning, the [currency converter](/conversion/currency-converter/) shows the **mid-market rate** (what banks see), not the retail rate. The page explains the typical 0.5-7% gap between mid-market and what you'll actually pay through different channels — useful before you commit to a remittance, swipe a card abroad, or accept a foreign payment.
