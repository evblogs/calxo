Calxo's insurance calculators help you size **the right cover for your family** and find what the actual premium will look like. Every tool uses median market rates for FY 2025-26 across the four largest term insurers (LIC, HDFC Life, Max Life, Tata AIA) for term insurance, and IRDAI-published statistics for health and motor.

## What you can calculate here

- **Term insurance premium** for any age, sum assured, term, gender and smoker status
- **Cover-to-premium ratio** so you can see how much protection each rupee of premium buys
- **Total premium over the policy term** for budgeting

## Why these are different

Most online "insurance calculators" are lead-generation forms that redirect you to a broker. Calxo's calculator runs entirely in your browser and shows you the math behind the estimate, with no signup or callback.

The premium estimates are based on actual published rate cards from FY 2025-26 and median underwriting outcomes. Your individual quote will vary by 10-20% based on medical history, family history, BMI, occupation and city, but the calculator gives you a realistic working number for budgeting and comparison.
