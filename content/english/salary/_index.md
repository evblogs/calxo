Calxo's salary calculators help you read your CTC like your finance team does — separating what you actually take home from what's just a number on the offer letter. Every tool is built on the actual statute (Income Tax Act sections, EPF Act, Payment of Gratuity Act 1972), with current FY 2025-26 thresholds.

## What you can calculate here

- **HRA exemption** under section 10(13A) for metro and non-metro renters
- **Take-home salary** from CTC, after PF, professional tax and TDS (coming soon)
- **Gratuity** under the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972 (coming soon)
- **Leave encashment** at retirement or resignation (coming soon)
- **In-hand salary breakdown** with all standard salary components

## Why these are different

Most CTC calculators online inflate take-home by ignoring TDS, professional tax (state-specific), or by using outdated standard deduction values. Calxo's tools use FY 2025-26 numbers and apply each calculation per the rule that actually governs it — not a simplified rule of thumb.

If you're negotiating an offer or restructuring a salary slip, start with the [HRA calculator](/salary/hra-calculator/) — for most renters in tier-1 cities, that's the single biggest tax-relevant component to get right.
